How to Order

  • Step#1
    Choose your order on our menu section.

  • Step#2
    Click on the messaging icon and provide the details of your order, our representative will assist you right away.

  • Step#3
    Once the order details are completed, our representative will call you for confirmation.

Note

  • Note#1
    Our delivery coverage is within Metro Manila only, We deliver Grab Rate COD and bank transfer for payment method Minimum order P500.

  • Note#2
    Our office hours are Monday to Sunday 8am-7pm, for holidays and events you can book your order 2-3 days prior to your desired date.

  • Note#3
    For inquiries, you can text or call us on this number 0917-896-9645 or you can send us an email at makisuhiexpressph@gmail.com.
